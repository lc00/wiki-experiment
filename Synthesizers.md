## Oscillator

* Generates raw sound
* Can combine to make more complex sounds

### Wave Types

#### Sine

* Pure

#### Sawtooth

* Harsh

#### Triangle

* Complex but mellower

#### Square

* Lofi, beepy-boopy
* A little harshness

## Filter

Alters sound.

* Has "cutoff" control that sets the frequency.
* Has "resonance" control that sets gain

* Low-pass
* Band-pass (cutoff is center frequency)
* High-pass

## Amplifier

* Generates output
